CRICKET LIKE NEVER BEFORE.

CricClubs is one of the leading cricket technology platforms which helps all the stakeholders of cricket irrespective of their roles such as administrator, player, scorer, umpires,fans to experience cricket like never before.

    Products

    League Management

    CricClubs league management solution is a complete package for cricket admins to smoothly conduct their leagues with ease. The entire league management setup can be completed within 5 mins.

    Live Scoring App

    With the CricClubs app, matches can be scored live through Android, iOS smartphones and also through any PC. The live matches can be shared through social media, anyone across the globe can follow the matches for free.

    Live Stream

    Take your match video to the next level with live stream technology and reach out to more audiences. Live match scores and cool graphics can be integrated into any live video easily in real time.

    Custom Apparel/Balls

    CricClubs manufactures high-quality customizable uniform kits and various types of Cricket balls at very affordable prices.

    LED Scoreboard

    Need to keep track of your scores during matches? The LED Scoreboard retrieves scores from the CricClubs servers and displays them live on the field.

    Academy Management

    CricClubs academy management solution provides academy administrators all the tools to professionally manage and run a cricket academy.

    Why Cricclubs

    CricClubs is a leading cricket technology company that is used in more than 58+countries and 30000+ leagues along with 35+ NGB's across the world. CricClubs is known for ease of navigation, comprehensive league management and is absolutely loved by our users. CricClubs believes that your league information, matches, scorecards, statistics etc should be freely and easily accessible by your players, fans, and audience.
